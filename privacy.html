Privacy Policy

At The Reboot, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information from visitors to our blog.

Information We Collect

The only information we collect is the email address you voluntarily provide through our contact form or newsletter sign-ups. We do not collect any other personal information automatically.

How We Use Your Email

We use your email address solely to respond to inquiries or to send you updates about our blog if you have signed up for the newsletter. We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties.

Cookies and Analytics

We do not use cookies or track personal data beyond the email you provide. Any analytics data we use is anonymous and purely for improving the blog experience.

How You Can Control Your Information

You can request to unsubscribe from our emails or have your email removed from our records at any time by contacting us at reboot-blogmovies@outlook.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
